Adopt A Pet

Adopt A Pet is a humane shelter that houses a variety of pets that are available for adoption. If you're in search for your forever best friend such as dogs, cats, or if you are looking for something different we have reptiles or ferrets you can come look at!

Why Adopt A Pet

Since 1980, when Adopt A Pet first established, 100% of the donations we receive go towards keeping our pets nourished, sheltered, healthy, and well cared for. We strive to take care of our own until a lucky family like yourself decides to adopt them!
